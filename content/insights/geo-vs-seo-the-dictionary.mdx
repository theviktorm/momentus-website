The most common question in our intake calls is some version of "isn't GEO just structured data done well?" It is not. SEO optimizes a brand for a ranking algorithm whose unit of output is a list of blue links. GEO optimizes a brand for a recommendation algorithm whose unit of output is a sentence. Lists tolerate ten winners; sentences tolerate one or two. That is a different game with a different scoreboard, and the team that confuses the two ships content that wins neither.
